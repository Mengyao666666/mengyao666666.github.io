{{< project-title text="Why Global Diversification Matters" >}}

A Tableau-based macro-financial visualization project studying global diversification across macro regimes. The project uses quantamental indicators and asset-return data to compare regional macro mechanisms, including inflation pressure, credit expansion, real-rate conditions, duration returns, FX forward returns, and equity index futures returns.
